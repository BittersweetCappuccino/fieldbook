Two views of the same product

This screen map shows where every screen lives in the hierarchy. Its companion, the routing board, shows how a record moves through each department — the action on every arrow. Both are traced from the mockup's real navigation.

Thumbnails are generated from a handful of reusable wireframe templates; the department stripe and filename on each come from the same FB_ENTITY_META map the app uses.
